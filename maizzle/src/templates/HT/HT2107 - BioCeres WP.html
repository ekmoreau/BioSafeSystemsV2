Implementing BioCeres WP, Beauveria bassiana bioinsecticide, into IPM programs can greatly reduce pest populations.

You’ve optimized your plants’ health and regularly scout for early signs and symptoms of pest infestations, but despite this, insects have set up shop. Now what?

For high pest populations, BioCeres WP can be combined with most conventional synthetic or organic insecticides for additional control and resistance management. Less extensive infestations can be controlled solely by BioCeres WP or partnered with biological organisms such as predatory mites, green lacewings, or parasitic wasps.
